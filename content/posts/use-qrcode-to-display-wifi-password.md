## Why

- Often forget the password
- Use a long random password to enhance security
- Easy to connect

## Qr Code Format

```md
WIFI:T:WPA;S:<SSID>;P:<PASSWORD>;;
```

## Minimum OS Requirements

- Android 10
- IOS 11
